SolenOS

SolenOS surfaces only three things.

  • What matters now.
  • What can wait.
  • What may become serious.

That's it.
No systems to manage. No complexity to learn. No additional burden.

SolenOS does not track. It relieves.

When everything is in your head at once, SolenOS helps you see what to deal with first.

  • You type messy care thoughts — anything you're handling right now
  • It groups and sorts them based on urgency and real-world risk
  • It shows you a simple next-action order — not a task list

Not reminders. Not planning. Just clarity when things feel overwhelming.

What SolenOS is

Before you sign up, we want to be clear about what this is — and what it's not.

SolenOS is not
  • A reminder app
  • A notes or journaling tool
  • A chatbot for caregiving questions
SolenOS is
  • A continuous care record for one person you are caring for
It connects
  • Events — falls, hospital visits, confusion episodes
  • Decisions — medication changes, care actions taken
  • Outcomes — what worked, what didn't
  • Time-based history — what happened before, and when

So nothing important gets lost over time.
